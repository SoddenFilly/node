Armi - Domina is a procedurally-generated strategy game which pits two players, each playing as the leader of their nation, against each other in a battle of strategy, positioning, and brute strength. Players are dropped down far away from one another on a randomly-generated world which includes many different biomes and gatherable resources. They must stockpile materials and weapons, recruit AI allies, and build up forts and cities to gain the edge over the other player. After a grace period, the two players are allowed to attack each other, and the match ends when one of the players is killed. Each player uses their fortifications and armies to eliminate the other player before they get eliminated themselves.

Armi is completely free to play, and will take nothing from you but a bit of your time. To start playing, simply click or tap the "login" button on the right-hand side of the top navbar (your right, not its). If you don't already have an account, you can make a new one. All you need is an email, a username, and a password. Once you've inputted this information, you'll have an account!

And my account? How do I access it once I've made it?

Once you've created your account, you can click on the little head symbol in the middle of the navbar at the top of the screen, which will bring up your profile. Here you can edit your bio (an optional paragraph of text describing yourself), change your username if needed, or view your stats, such as how many games you've played, wins and losses you've tallied, and what achievements you've gotten. You can also pick an avatar that will be displayed to others using the chat function.
